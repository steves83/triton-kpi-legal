Privacy Policy

Triton KPI Dashboard is an internal reporting tool used by Triton North to read accounting data from connected QuickBooks Online companies and display analytics in dashboards.

Information we collect

  • QuickBooks Online accounting data that the authorized user allows the app to access (for example: chart of accounts, general ledger transactions, vendors, customers, and related reporting fields).
  • Technical logs needed to operate the service (for example: timestamps, error logs, and refresh status).

How we use information

  • To produce internal financial and operational reporting.
  • To maintain and improve the dashboards and data pipelines.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is used for internal reporting and may be accessible to authorized Triton North personnel and approved service providers supporting hosting and operations.

Data retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for reporting and operational purposes, and may be deleted upon request where applicable.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect data.
